ABOUT

Amanda Delaplaine is a rising Senior at the University of Miami pursuing a B.B.A in Business Technology and a minor in Computer Science. Amanda was born and raised in Miami Beach, Florida.

She first became interested in technology when joining her high school’s robotics club. In high school, Amanda enrolled in an introductory programming class which later lead her to take AP Computer Science. Throughout high school, Amanda volunteered at the Lotus House, a woman’s shelter in Miami, Florida, and taught basic technology courses to children ages 10-15 on a weekly basis. This lead to Amanda’s nomination for the Silver Knight Award in Vocational Technology.

In addition to pursuing her degree at the University of Miami, Amanda is currently a member of the Google Developer Student Club and the National Collegiate Honor Society. Amanda currently serves as a Teaching Assistant for Introduction to Business Technology. Additionally, throughout her time at the University of Miami, Amanda has worked as a Wealth Management Intern at Morgan Stanley, Gallery Director at Hibiscus Gallery, and Creative Executive at Sentient Entertainment.
